Depression

A group of conditions associated with the elevation or lowering of a person's mood, such as depression or bipolar disorder MOST COMMON TYPES Clinical depression A mental health disorder characterised by persistently depressed mood or loss of interest in activities, causing significant impairment in daily life. Persistent depressive disorder A mild but long-term form of depression. Bipolar disorder A disorder associated with episodes of mood swings ranging from depressive lows to manic highs. Bipolar II disorder A type of bipolar disorder characterised by depressive and hypomanic episodes. Postpartum depression Depression that occurs after childbirth.
